## Purpose

This batch replaces the earlier Diaochan foundation card direction. The previous V1-V3 Diaochan cards leaned too much into the shared jade/teal ink palette and did not match the existing Diaochan standee. Future Diaochan cards should use this red-aligned contract instead.

## Diaochan Red-Aligned Contract

- Palette: deep cinnabar red, warm white robe fabric, black ink, black hair, restrained aged gold.
- Character anchors: round fan, flowing white sleeves, red silk ribbons, court-dancer wuxia elegance.
- Avoid: jade, teal, cyan, blue-green, emerald, neon green.
- Preserve the global card contract: 1024x1536 portrait, upper-middle subject, lower 28 percent safe for runtime text/chrome, no text, no symbols, no UI frame.
